                                                          May 9, 1958

                          AKUMAGLO v. UNITED AFRICA CO. (GOLD COAST) LTD.

                                                                     Court of Appeal

.                             Korsah C.J., Granville Sharp J.A., Ollennu J.

 

 

On appeal the decision of Smith Ag.J. (as he then was) given on April 3, 1956, and reported at 1 W.A.L.R. 217, was reversed on the grounds that the learned judge had misdirected himself on the evidence. On the evidence the force of the impact of the pontoon against the river bank had caused the former to tilt and this was the reason for the plaintiff's lorry running backwards from the pontoon into the river. Such handling of the pontoon by the defendant's servants was held to be negligent.

Apaloo for the plaintiff.

Akufo Addo for the defendant.
