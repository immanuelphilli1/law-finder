October 22,1956.

                                                                                PRACTICE NOTE

                                               Supreme Court of the Gold Coast ,Ashanti Division, Land Court, Kumasi

                                                                                  Manyo-Plange J

 
 

Practice-Appeals from Native Courts-Application for extension of time to appeal- Method of making application.

During the course of an action, MAKYO·PLAKGE J., following the rule in Evennett v. Lawrence (1) and Absi v. Mends (2), held that on an application for extension of time to appeal to the Supreme Court from a Native Court, the respondent should be given the opportunity of being heard, so as to give him the opportunity of objecting. The application must therefore be made on notice and not ex parte.

   Cases referred to:

(1) Evennett v. Lawrence (1876) 4 Ch.D. 139 .

(2) Absi v. Mends (1935) 2 W.A.C.A. 238.

S. G. D.
