 Appeal Court, 30th May. 1940

Appeal from a judgment of Provincial Commissioner setting aside award of Paramount Tribunal of the Agona State-proceedings in both lower Courts unsatisfactory-Constitution of Commis­sioner's Court altered during hearing-case sent back for rehearing to the Native Tribunal with a strong recommendation for transfer to Divisional Court.

Held: (1) The trial in the Native Tribunal was unsatisfactory because (a) difficult questions arose upon documents in English form and (b) the question turned largely upon exact measurements.

(2) The appeal in the Provincial Commissioner's Court was unsatisfactory, as having been heard partly by one individual and partly by another. and for other reasons.

(3) Both judgments are set aside, and the case remitted to the Native Tribunal for hearing de novo. Strong recommendation for transfer to Divisional Court under Section 75 of the N.A. Ordinance (Cap. 76).

There is no need to set out the facts.
